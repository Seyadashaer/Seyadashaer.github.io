I'm a Data Analyst and Business Intelligence Specialist with a background in medicine. Over the past two years, I've been building reporting systems, clinical registries, and Power BI dashboards for hospital networks in Palestine — translating raw clinical and operational data into insights that support executive decision-making and hospital performance management.

My medical degree gives me an uncommon advantage: I understand the data I work with — ICD codes, clinical workflows, EHR structures, and the real-world meaning behind the numbers. That foundation shapes how I design data systems and communicate findings to both clinical and non-clinical stakeholders.

I hold certifications in Microsoft Power BI (PL-300), Certified Health Data Analyst (CHDA), and MEAL DPro, and I've completed nanodegrees in both Data Analysis and Data Science. I also have a software development background, which means I can work directly with databases, write clean SQL, and build end-to-end data pipelines without relying on intermediaries.

I'm drawn to work at the intersection of healthcare, data quality, and organizational learning — where better data leads to better care.
